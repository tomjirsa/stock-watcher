Which TA indicators do you want to add?

These will become both signal generators and chart overlays. Select all that interest you.

A

MACD

Moving Average Convergence/Divergence — signals trend changes via MACD line crossing the signal line. Shown as a separate panel below the price chart.

Signal: MACD crossover (bullish when MACD crosses above signal line)

B

Bollinger Bands

Upper/lower bands ±2 std devs from a 20-day MA. Squeeze (bands narrowing) signals a potential breakout. Overlaid directly on the price chart.

Signal: Band squeeze or price touching lower band (mean reversion)

C

RSI Visual Panel

RSI(14) is already used in MomentumStrategy — this adds a dedicated RSI panel on the chart so you can see it visually. No new strategy needed.

Visual only — RSI logic already exists in MomentumStrategy

D

VWAP

Volume-Weighted Average Price — key intraday level used by institutions. Price above VWAP = bullish bias. Overlaid on the price chart.

Signal: Price crossing above VWAP with strong volume

You can select multiple. Or type your own in the terminal.
